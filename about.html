Who We Are

We are a 100% export-oriented garments manufacturing factory with a full setup of six sewing lines. We are a challenging entrepreneur and always committed to the highest quality and prompt delivery. We are arranged with Two Hundred plus number of manpower including experienced staff and skilled workers.

Our Mission

To be the icon in leading creativity for meeting our customers' needs and sustainable growth. We help international buyer's source garments and home item from all regions of Bangladesh.

Our Vision

With a firm grounding in the dynamics of textile and garment business, we identify our client needs, in turn, the end consumer needs, and recognize the opportunities. Our merchandising & sourcing department efficiently sourcing fabric, yarn, trims & accessories from India, China and Turkey globally for our customer.
